📘 2. Grammar Notes: Present Simple

Super simple rule

I / You / We / They + verb: I wake up. You play games.

He / She / It + verb + s: She wakes up. He plays games.

Turkish note: “Her gün yaptığımız şeyler” için Present Simple kullanırız. Örnek: I brush my teeth. = Dişlerimi fırçalarım.

✅ Positive

I eat breakfast.

She eats breakfast.

❌ Negative

I don’t play at night.

He doesn’t play at night.

❓ Questions

Do you wake up early?

Does she go to school?

💬 Short answers

Yes, I do. / No, I don’t.

Yes, she does. / No, she doesn’t.

Frequency words

always 100% · usually often · sometimes not always · never 0%

⚠️ Common mistakes

❌ She wake up. ✅ She wakes up.

❌ He don’t eat. ✅ He doesn’t eat.

❌ Does you play? ✅ Do you play?

👩‍🏫 Teacher tips

Use hand signs: one finger for subject, one for verb, add “s” with a snake sound: sssss. Make students clap the sentence rhythm.
